How to Use

  1. Click "Start Recording" to record your voice beatboxing
  2. Click "Stop Recording" when finished - your track will appear below
  3. Repeat to add multiple layers of beats
  4. Adjust volume and BPM (beats per minute) to your preference
  5. Click "Play All" to hear your layered beatbox loop
  6. Use "Download MP3" to save your creation
